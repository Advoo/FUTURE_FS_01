Who I am

Not a coder.
A market observer.

"I look at what the market has left unanswered, ask why — then design the simplest, most astonishing response."

I'm Advocate Karabo Tladi — a Computer Science student at the University of the Western Cape, aspiring AI Engineer, and one of eight directors at Aution, Cape Town's privately-held intelligent mobility company. I operate at the intersection of systems, people, and market opportunity.

My approach to technology isn't driven by passion for programming. It's driven by a question: why hasn't this been done yet? I look at the market, find the gap, and design an execution that feels inevitable in hindsight — simple, elegant, and built for the people it actually serves.

Currently building toward an AI engineering practice where intelligence isn't a product you open — it's infrastructure that disappears into human experience, quietly enhancing every decision. If you're working at that edge, let's think together →
